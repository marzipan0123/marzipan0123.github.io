Goal Keeper Academy

Dynasty Goalkeeping Academy is an innovative goalkeeper camp experience. It is the ultimate experience for elite goalkeepers. Tracy Noonan has created a unique Academy that will provide elite keepers with the necessary tools and training to take their play to the next level. Tracy’s expertise from her years as a top-level collegiate, national team, and professional player will allow her to share crucial insights into all aspects of goalkeeping; from the proper nutritional outlook, to on-the-field technical training, mental skills development, to a weight training program that will maximize a goalkeeper’s performance.

Tracy will personally introduce you to her first-class work ethic that can help you maximize your goalkeeping skills if you are willing to work as hard as she does! This Academy will be intense, so come prepared to train on your edge, compete with the best, and become a part of a powerful goalkeeper network of friends for life — Dynasty Goalkeeping.

Dynasty Goalkeeping Academy will run 5 weeks of camp at Saint Mary’s School in Raleigh, NC during the summer of 2020. It is a 5 day residential program designed for the college bound, elite goalkeeper. We accept females age 13 or older (students must turn 13 in 2020, hence a ’07 birth year) with firm age guidelines for each week. Each week of camp will be limited to 12 students to maximize results and to create a personalized experience for each individual.

Goal Keeper Clinics

Dynasty Goalkeeping is available to run clinics at various times during the year throughout the country. These are a great opportunity for students that may not be able to travel to North Carolina during the summer, (due to scheduling conflicts or expenses), to get a feel for the Dynasty Goalkeeping Academy atmosphere. They are also a great way to tune-up skills over the winter, in the off season, or between camps offered during the summer.

Private Overnight and Weekend Mini-Camp

Private overnight and weekend ‘mini-camps’ can also be arranged for students to fit their schedules and needs. This unique opportunity allows for a complete immersion into goalkeeping and he Dynasty Goalkeeping philosophy. These mini-camps provide training specific to the student’s needs, personalized goal setting, nutritional guidance, mentorship, and more in depth video analysis than can be provided in the summer camp environment. This is an excellent option to consider for students who are too young to attend the summer camp (under the age of 14) or who are too busy with club, ODP, and personal commitments during the summer. Please contact Tracy for more details on arranging a private mini-camp.

All training will be customized to help players maximize their potential and advance towards their goals. This includes:

  • Physical, technical, mental, and tactical aspects of the game
  • College guidance
  • Strength and conditioning work
  • Nutritional advice
